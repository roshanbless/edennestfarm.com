Our Story

Eden Nest Farm

Nestled in the lush green hills of Inchappara, Pathanamthitta, Eden Nest Farm was built on one simple belief — every family deserves the freshest, most natural eggs from hens raised with genuine care.

We are a family-run farm rooted in honest, sustainable practices. Our hens are fed natural feed, given open space, and never given hormones or antibiotics. What you get is nature in its purest form.

When you choose Eden Nest Farm, you support local farming, your family's health, and a tradition built on faith and care.
